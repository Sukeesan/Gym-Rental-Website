ABOUT US

We love sports. Our passion and love for sports inspired us to build a worldclass indoor sports rental facility for everyone to enjoy! At EssKay Sportplex, we do not believe in cutting corners, which means you will only find cutting-edge equipment, top-notch multipurpose vinyl flooring, supreme padded turf that reduces impact stress on joints and decreases injuries, and regulation-sized nets for basketball, volleyball, and soccer!



OUR FACILITY



EssKay Sportsplex offers guests an indoor sports facility that houses 4 courts and 2 soccer fields for rent.

Our facility is available for rent on a first come first serve basis. We also work with organized sports leagues wishing to reserve our facility for multiple games and practices throughout the season.
